Tic Tac Toe

Rules:

  1. The first player will have symbol X.
  2. The goal of the game is to get 3 symbols in a row
  3. You play by clicking into the box that you want to occupy with your symbol
  4. You win the game when you have 3 of your symbols in a row horizontally, vertically, or diagonally in either direction
  5. Each cell can only be occupied by one symbol.
  6. The status will tell you who the current player is and who won.
